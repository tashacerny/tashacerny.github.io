Tasha Cerny is a project manager, content creator, and storyteller who 
brings collaboration, organization, and creativity to every endeavor. 
She keeps projects moving, communication flowing, and details in order — 
while never losing sight of the people behind the work.

Tasha has led projects of all sizes across entertainment, education, 
digital media, healthcare, politics, and non-profits. On a daily basis 
she manages timelines, content calendars, deliverables, and expectations, 
all with a smile. Her strength is seeing the forest for the trees — 
ensuring everyone moves forward with clarity, teamwork, and a focus on 
shared goals.

Her background is largely rooted in the entertainment industry, where she 
worked in creative development, short-form production, research, writing, 
and marketing. She has collaborated with directors, writers, producers, 
actors, and creative teams to advance and fund projects from page to 
screen. That experience sharpened her adaptability under tight deadlines 
and budgets, and her ability to communicate across a wide range of creative 
personalities to find shared goals.

Social media is at the heart of Tasha's marketing experience. She has 
built and executed content strategies that increase visibility, drive 
engagement, and elevate brand voices across LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, 
TikTok, and X. A writer at her core, she understands how to combine story 
and brand to build stronger digital relationships and grow an audience — 
creatively and strategically, and never boring.

If you're looking for a project manager who keeps teams happy and 
productive, someone to build or scale a brand, or a collaborative partner 
for a creative endeavor — Tasha would love to connect. Feel free to ask 
her about her three-legged dog while you're at it. 🐾
